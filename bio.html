Who is Bailey?

Bailey is a software developer from Austin, Texas. She grew up obsessing over sporcle logic puzzles and feels the same euphoric rush of understanding when she gets the code to work as she did when she figured out which countries belonged in which boxes.
In her spare time she knits, cycles, and listens to Warren Zevon and Townes Van Zandt. Her favorite authors are Roberto Bolano and W. G. Sebald and she is constantly looking for people who want to talk about their books. If you like either of them, please, please reach out.
